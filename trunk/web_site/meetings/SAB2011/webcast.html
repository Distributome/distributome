Web-stream of the 2011 Distributome Project SAB Meeting

The 2011 Distributome Project Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) Meeting took place on Friday Oct. 28, 2011, 8:00-10:00 AM (Pacific Time). The meeting was by-invitation only and aimed to showcase the Distributome project progress to-date, solicit constructive and critical feedback from the SAB board, and chart the plans for the project over the next year.
