Distributome Resource Licenses

The Distributome Project is an open-source, open content-development project for exploring, discovering, navigating, learning and computational utilization of diverse probability distributions, which are functions that assign probabilities to events and give rise to mass or density functions, distribution functions, quantile functions, probability and moment generating functions.
