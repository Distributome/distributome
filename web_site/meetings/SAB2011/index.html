2011 Distributome Project Scientific Advisory Board Meeting

The 2011 Distributome Project Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) Meeting takes place on Friday Oct. 28, 2011, 8:00-10:00 AM (Pacific Time). The meeting is by-invitation only. The three broader goals of the meeting are to showcase the Distributome project progress to-date, solicit constructive and critical feedback from the SAB board, and chart the plans for the project over the next year.

Logistics
  • Timer, Place, Venue: Friday Oct. 28, 2011, 8:00-10:00 AM (Pacific Time). Virtual Ready-Talk web-meeting.
  • Participants:
    • Distributome SAB members: Lawrence Moore (Duke), David Aldous (Berkeley) and Robert Dobrow (Carleton College)
    • Distributome PIs: Ivo Dinov (SOCR/UCLA), Dennis Pearl (MBI/OSU) and Kyle Siegrist (UAH/VLPS)
  • Join the Meeting: Instructions will be emailed to all invitees
Agenda
  • Introductions
  • What is the Distributome Project?
  • Distributome Website: as an open-access and open-development  resource (Navigator)
  • Technical foundations: XML meta-data (DB), HTML DB Validator, XSD schema, Distributome LGPL-licensed source code, development page
  • Distributome as an educational resource: Distributome Wiki activities
  • Plans for Distributome User Interface Redesign: Navigator (1, 2), Editor (1, 2, 3)
  • SAB Vision and Directives
    • Approaches to engage the larger community in expanding, supporting and improving the Distributome resources (sustainability)
    • Best Practices of project evaluation and assessment of the project's broader impact
    • How to integrate Distributome data, tools, activities, examples, problems, solutions?
    • Other
  • Open Discussion
  • Adjourn
